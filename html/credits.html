Sudokool V 2.5 - 15 Feb 2009

The Story so far ...

It all started back in 2005 when I was learning Core Java 1.4, after completion of my Engineering in Electronics and Communications. I needed a small project to brush up and practise all the knowledge gained. Sudoku puzzles had gained so popularity that they were part of all the newspapers and magazines since then. It was a dual challenge for me : a sudoku solver algorithm and a GUI interface.

Sudukool was launched on 25th Sep 2005. It was just a soduku solver. There were 100 puzzles in 3 difficulty levels. I had made the solver algorithm after studying the sudoku solving techniques. The biggest limitation was that it didn't have sudoku puzzle generation.

Due to the busy professional life, it was only after 3 years, that I thought to upgrade Sudokool to V2. Sudokool is now not limited in any terms and has all the features of a commercial counterpart.
